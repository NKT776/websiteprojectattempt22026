Fighting Game Mains 2026

I play quite a few fighting games, and in many of those games I can't stick to simply playing one character. I usually have at least 3 characters that I play in each game that I put any amount of time into, so I've compiled some lists of characters I particularly enjoy, by order of preference.

While I dabble in newer games (such as UNI2) I play a lot of older games, so I choose a background for this page that reflects more old-school web design. I also find this background kind of charming, even if it shows its age.
